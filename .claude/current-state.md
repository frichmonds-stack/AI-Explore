- **The delivery rubric skeleton is SETTLED (2026-07-23, session 2) — `Function / Form / Substance`**, two-tier, superseding the old "6 flat delivery criteria" framing. **Function** = does it perform as meant (works/runs · usable · editable · technical integrity · interactivity); **Form** = does it reach the senses right (layout · notation/form conventions · legibility & audibility · aesthetic); **Substance** = is it correct *and any good* (accuracy · alignment · completeness · sequence · **craft**/anti-slop).
